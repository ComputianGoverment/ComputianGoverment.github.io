President Announces ₢2.4 Billion Digital Infrastructure Investment

President Dr. Alan K. Turing-Patel today announced the most ambitious technology modernization program in Computia's history. The National Digital Advancement Program (NDAP) 2009 will bring high-speed internet connectivity to all 12 provinces, establish new technology parks in Byteburg, Port Recursive, and Silicon Delta, and modernize all government services through the new e-Gov 2.0 platform.

"Computia was founded on the principles of innovation and progress. This program ensures we remain at the forefront of the digital revolution," said President Turing-Patel during the announcement at the National Assembly.
